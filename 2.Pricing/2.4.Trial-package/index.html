Trial package plans#

The trial package provides support for evaluation purposes or proof-of-concept activities, transition pathway to sustained access, new user onboarding and assessment.

This is free but time-limited (up to 4 months) access to CLIMB resources which includes:

  • 10 vCPUs
  • 500GB Team Shared Storage
  • 500GB S3 Storage

Please contact us for to discuss what CLIMB can offer and a quote.

Warning

Once this free trial period ends, your team will be disabled and you will no longer be able to access resources unless payment plans are put in place.
